# Product Overview

## Working Title
Affiliate Storefront *(name to be confirmed during onboarding)*

## What It Is
A zero-inventory e-commerce affiliate storefront platform. Affiliate marketers and content creators build a branded, curated product catalog where every item links out to an external retailer (Amazon, Shopify, brand sites, or any external store). Visitors discover and browse products, click through to buy on the external store, and the affiliate earns a commission — no fulfillment, no inventory, no payment processing.

## Users & Personas
- **Affiliate Marketer** — runs niche product sites monetized via affiliate programs (Amazon Associates, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, etc.). Wants a professional, high-converting home for curated product recommendations.
- **Content Creator / Influencer** — recommends products to an audience and needs a clean, branded hub that centralizes all affiliate links without relying on generic third-party link trees.
- **Store Admin** — the operator managing the catalog: adding products, updating affiliate URLs, organizing collections, and reviewing click performance.

## Positioning
Zero-inventory affiliate storefront: curate products, link to external stores, earn commissions. The competitive advantage is curation quality and a professional user experience — not logistics or fulfillment.

## Brand & Tone
Modern, conversion-focused, trustworthy. Clean and editorial — the storefront should feel premium so visitors trust the recommendations and click through confidently.

## Core Workflow
1. Admin adds products: title, image, description, and affiliate URL pointing to the external retailer
2. Products are organized into categories and collections
3. Visitors browse the branded storefront and discover curated products
4. Visitor clicks "Shop Now" / "Buy on [Retailer]" → redirected to the external store via the affiliate link
5. Purchase on the external store earns a commission for the affiliate owner

## Key Features
- **Product Catalog Management** — add, edit, and organize products with full affiliate link configuration
- **Affiliate Link Manager** — configure outbound URLs per product with affiliate parameter support
- **Category & Collection Organization** — group products for browsability and discoverability
- **Conversion-Optimized Storefront** — clean, fast, product-first UI designed to maximize click-throughs to external stores
- **Click-Through Tracking** — log and review affiliate link click counts per product for performance visibility
- **External Store Integration** — link to any retailer: Amazon, Shopify stores, WooCommerce, or any brand site

## Strategic Principles
- The only revenue event is the affiliate click-through — every design decision serves that outcome
- No checkout, no cart, no payment processing — the experience hands off cleanly to the external store
- Curation quality is the competitive moat; the platform makes it easy to maintain a polished, up-to-date catalog
- A professional branded storefront builds visitor trust and drives higher click-through conversion vs. generic link trees
